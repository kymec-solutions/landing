# Kymec

Kymec is a premium digital engineering and product studio. We are a senior, remote, multidisciplinary team that combines technology, design, and precise execution to turn ideas into real products.

## What We Do

- Full-stack development (web apps, platforms, and custom products)
- Mobile apps and IoT
- Technical consulting and architecture
- Experience and product design

## Our Process

1) Schedule a meeting
2) Listen to the idea and goals
3) Build a clear quote with scope and timeline
4) Develop the solution
5) Implement in production
6) Test in real environments
7) Provide support if needed

## How We Work

- Direct communication with the engineering team
- Clear and transparent scope
- Iterative delivery
- Focus on performance, quality, and scalability

## Team

A senior team with international experience in software, product, and operations. We work 100% remotely between Argentina and Poland.
